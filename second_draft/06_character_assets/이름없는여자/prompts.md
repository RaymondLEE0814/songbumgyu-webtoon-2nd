## 01_reference_sheet
Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: webtoon supernatural character reference sheet
Primary request: Create a reference sheet for the Nameless Girl, a Korean high school girl-shaped residual information structure from a 1999 abandoned subway incident.
Scene/backdrop: minimal pale gray background with faint CCTV frame edges and frozen scanline texture.
Subject: teenage Korean girl in wet old school uniform from the late 1990s, pale still face, eyes not making contact, one hand holding a torn subway ticket, posture perfectly fixed as if a paused video frame.
Style/medium: Korean webtoon mystery horror concept art, semi-realistic, restrained, uncanny stillness.
Composition/framing: front view, 3/4 view, side view, close-up of torn ticket, close-up of frozen water droplets and pixel glitches around edges.
Lighting/mood: cold fluorescent, silent, static, eerie but not aggressive.
Color palette: desaturated school uniform, wet gray, pale skin, white-blue static, small black barcode artifacts.
Constraints: she must feel like an event leaking through reality, not a vengeful ghost; keep motion minimal.
Avoid: attacking pose, screaming face, gore, long-hair jump scare cliché, monster teeth, magic aura, watermark.
